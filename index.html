Our Mission

Bridging the gap between modern technology and senior citizens.

GenTech is a nonprofit organization that serves communities across the DMV Area. Our primary mission is to bridge the gap between modern technology and its usage by senior citizens.

At GenTech, we believe that teaching the ability to use technology to senior citizens will help them further connect with family and friends, continue lifelong learning, maintain greater independence in their daily lives, improve quality of life, and accomplish more than what they thought would be possible.

What We Teach

Practical skills seniors can use right away.

GenTech lessons are hands-on, friendly, and focused on the technology seniors actually use in daily life.

📱

Phones & Tablets

Texting, calling, FaceTime, contacts, photos, apps, settings, and accessibility tools.

💬

Staying Connected

Video calls, messaging, sharing photos, email basics, and communicating with family.

🔐

Online Safety

Passwords, scam awareness, suspicious messages, privacy settings, and safe browsing.

🗓️

Daily Tools

Calendars, reminders, maps, transportation apps, online forms, and useful websites.
